<p><p>Medical metal tubing market includes various types such as stainless steel, titanium, and nitinol (NiTi). Stainless steel tubing is known for its durability and resistance to corrosion, making it suitable for a wide range of medical applications. Titanium tubing is lightweight, strong, and biocompatible, making it ideal for surgical implants and medical devices. Nitinol, a nickel-titanium alloy, is known for its shape memory and superelastic properties, making it useful for applications such as stents and orthodontic wires. Each type offers unique benefits for different medical purposes.</p></p>
