<p><p>Autocorrelators market includes various types such as Intensity Autocorrelators, Interferometric Autocorrelators, Scanning Autocorrelators, and Single-shot Autocorrelators. Intensity Autocorrelators measure the intensity profile of an input beam. Interferometric Autocorrelators utilize interferometry to measure the temporal properties of ultrashort laser pulses. Scanning Autocorrelators scan the delay between two pulses to determine the pulse width. Single-shot Autocorrelators provide instantaneous measurements of pulse duration without the need for repetitive measurements. These different types cater to different applications and requirements in the field of optics and laser technology.</p></p>
