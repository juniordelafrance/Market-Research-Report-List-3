Multiflexing Chain Conveyor utilizes multiple strands of chains to create a flexible and efficient conveying system. On the other hand, Drag Chain Conveyor uses a series of interconnected chain links to pull or drag material along the conveyor. Both types offer different advantages in terms of flexibility, load capacity, and application suitability in the market.</p></p>
