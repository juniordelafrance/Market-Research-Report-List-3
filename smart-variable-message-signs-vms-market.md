<p><p>Smart Variable Message Signs (VMS) come in two main market types: Fixed Type and Mobile Type. Fixed Type VMS are permanent displays installed in one location, typically along highways or urban roads. They provide essential information like traffic warnings, road conditions, and directions. Mobile Type VMS are portable and can be moved to different locations as needed. They are commonly used in construction zones, special events, or emergencies where temporary messaging is required. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring road safety and efficient traffic management.</p></p>
