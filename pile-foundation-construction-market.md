<p><ul><li>Hammering</li><li>Vibration Method</li><li>Press Method</li><li>Water Jet</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pile foundation construction market types refer to different methods used to drive piles into the ground to provide support for structures. The hammering method involves using a pile driver to force the piles into the soil, while the vibration method involves using vibrating equipment to achieve the same result. The press method uses hydraulic presses to insert piles, and the water jet method utilizes water jets to create holes for the piles. Other methods may include screw piles or helical piles.</p></p>
