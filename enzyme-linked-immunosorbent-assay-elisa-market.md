<p><p>Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) is a widely used method for detecting and quantifying antibodies or antigens in a sample. There are different types of ELISA tests, including Sandwich ELISA, Indirect ELISA, Competitive ELISA, and Others. In Sandwich ELISA, the antigen is sandwiched between two antibodies, while in Indirect ELISA, the antigen is detected using a secondary antibody. Competitive ELISA involves competition between the antigen and a labeled antigen for binding to an antibody. Other types of ELISA include direct ELISA and reverse ELISA.</p></p>
