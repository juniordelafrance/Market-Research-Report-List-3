<p><ul><li>H264</li><li>H265</li><li>VP9</li><li>AV1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video transcoding market types refer to the different compression technologies used for encoding and decoding video files. H.264, also known as AVC, is a widely used standard offering good quality and compression efficiency. H.265, or HEVC, improves upon H.264 by providing even better compression while maintaining quality. VP9 and AV1 are newer codecs developed by Google and Alliance for Open Media, respectively, which aim to further enhance compression efficiency and quality. Each of these codecs caters to different needs and preferences in the video transcoding market.</p></p>
