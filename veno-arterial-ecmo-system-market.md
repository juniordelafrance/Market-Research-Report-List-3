<p><p>A Veno-Arterial ECMO System is a medical device used to provide life support to patients experiencing severe heart and/or lung failure. It works by pumping blood from the patient's body through an oxygenator (artificial lung) to remove carbon dioxide and add oxygen, before returning the blood to the body. This system helps to temporarily support the patient's heart and/or lungs to allow time for recovery or for more permanent treatment options to be considered.</p><p>The Veno-Arterial ECMO System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period.
