<p><strong>The Fuel Leak Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positioning Type</li><li>Non-positioning Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel leak detection equipment can be classified into two main types: positioning type and non-positioning type. </p><p>Positioning type equipment uses sensors and alarms to detect leaks at specific points along fuel pipelines or storage tanks. Non-positioning type equipment, on the other hand, constantly monitors for leaks throughout the entire system using technologies like acoustic sensors or pressure sensors.</p><p>Both types of equipment are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficiency of fuel storage and transportation systems.</p></p>
