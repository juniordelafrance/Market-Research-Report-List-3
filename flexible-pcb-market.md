<p><strong>The Flexible PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li><li>Multi-Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible PCBs are classified into three main types: Single Layer, Double Layer, and Multi-Layer. Single Layer PCBs consist of one layer of conductive material, making them ideal for simple circuits. Double Layer PCBs have two layers of conductive material, allowing for more complex designs with components on both sides. Multi-Layer PCBs have three or more layers of conductive material, offering the highest level of complexity and functionality. Each type caters to different circuit design requirements, with Multi-Layer PCBs being the most versatile and capable of handling advanced electronics.</p></p>
