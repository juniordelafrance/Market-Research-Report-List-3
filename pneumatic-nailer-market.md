<p><p>Pneumatic nailers are available in two main types - coil-style and strip-style. Coil-style pneumatic nailers use a coiled magazine to hold nails, allowing for longer working periods without reloading. This type is preferred for larger projects. Strip-style pneumatic nailers use a long, straight magazine to hold nails and are typically used for smaller, precision-based projects. Both types offer efficient and powerful nail driving capabilities, making them popular choices among contractors and construction professionals.</p></p>
