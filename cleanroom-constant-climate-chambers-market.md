<p><p>Cleanroom constant climate chambers are specialized enclosures used for maintaining a controlled temperature and humidity level for various applications such as research, testing, and production. There are two main types of cleanroom constant climate chambers: box type and enter type. </p><p>Box type chambers are compact and self-contained units that can be easily placed on a bench or table. They are ideal for small-scale experiments and testing.</p><p>Enter type chambers are larger in size and have a walk-in design, making them suitable for more extensive research and production activities that require larger working spaces. Both types provide a controlled environment for precise and reliable testing and research processes.</p></p>
