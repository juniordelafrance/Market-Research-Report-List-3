<p><p>Bariatric surgical procedures market includes various types of surgeries such as Gastric Bypass Surgery, Adjustable Gastric Banding Surgery, Gastric Sleeve Surgery, Duodenal Switch Surgery, and others. Gastric Bypass Surgery involves creating a smaller stomach pouch. Adjustable Gastric Banding Surgery involves placing a band around the upper part of the stomach to create a smaller pouch. Gastric Sleeve Surgery involves removing a large portion of the stomach. Duodenal Switch Surgery combines stomach reduction with bypassing a large portion of the small intestine. Other procedures include gastric balloon, gastric plication, and biliopancreatic diversion.</p></p>
