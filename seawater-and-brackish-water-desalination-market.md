<p><p>Seawater and Brackish Water Desalination market consists of various types such as Multistage Flash Distillation, Multi-Effect Distillation, Vapor Compression, Reverse Osmosis, and Nanofiltration. Multistage Flash Distillation involves heating seawater and condensing the steam. Multi-Effect Distillation uses multiple chambers with decreasing pressure levels to distill water. Vapor Compression utilizes heat to evaporate water and separate impurities. Reverse Osmosis pushes seawater through a semi-permeable membrane to filter out salts. Nanofiltration is a similar process but with a finer membrane to remove smaller particles.</p></p>
