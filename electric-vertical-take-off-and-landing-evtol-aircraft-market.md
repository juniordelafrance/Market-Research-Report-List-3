<p><p>Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) aircraft can be classified into three main market types based on their propulsion systems and flight capabilities. Vectored Thrust eVTOLs use tilting rotors or fans for vertical take-off and forward flight. Multirotor eVTOLs have multiple rotors for vertical lift and maneuverability. Lift + Cruise eVTOLs combine fixed wings for efficient long-range cruise flight with vertical take-off and landing capability. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications for urban air mobility and other aerospace industries.</p></p>
