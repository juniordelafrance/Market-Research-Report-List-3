Single-stranded cDNA probes are synthesized from RNA via reverse transcription and are used for gene expression analysis. Oligonucleotide probes are short, synthetic DNA or RNA molecules with high specificity and are commonly used in various molecular biology techniques. Each type of probe serves specific purposes in gene expression and detection studies.</p></p>
