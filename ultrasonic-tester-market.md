<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li><li>Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li><li>TOFD Ultrasonic Testing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic testers are used for non-destructive testing in various industries. Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machines are the most traditional type, using a single transducer to send and receive sound waves. Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing Machines use multiple transducers to create detailed images, offering better defect detection. Time-of-Flight Diffraction (TOFD) Ultrasonic Testing Machines use diffraction patterns to detect defects accurately and are often used for weld inspections. Each type of ultrasonic tester has its advantages and applications in different industries for ensuring product quality and safety.</p></p>
