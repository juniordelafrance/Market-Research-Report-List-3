<p><strong>The Machine Tool Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Probe</li><li>Non-Contact Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine tool probes are used in various industries for precise measurements and quality control. Contact probes require physical contact with the workpiece to gather data, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Non-contact probes use technologies like lasers or vision systems to collect data without touching the workpiece, offering faster and more versatile measurement capabilities. Contact probes are popular in industries like automotive and aerospace, while non-contact probes are preferred in electronics and medical device manufacturing. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and accurate production processes.</p></p>
