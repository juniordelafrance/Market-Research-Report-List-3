<p><p>Barrier (Static) Filters are air filter systems that use physical barriers such as screens or meshes to trap particles in the air. Self-Cleaning Pulse Filters are systems that use pulses of compressed air to remove collected particles from the filter surface, ensuring continual efficiency. Other types of air filter systems may include electrostatic filters or media filters that use a layer of material to capture particles. Each type offers unique benefits and advantages for different industrial applications in the gas turbine industry.</p></p>
