<p><p>Ocean current energy electric generators are divided into two main market types: Horizontal Shaft Generator and Vertical Shaft Generator. Horizontal Shaft Generators are typically used in areas with high-speed currents and are designed to harness energy through a horizontal spinning motion. Vertical Shaft Generators, on the other hand, are better suited for areas with slower currents, using a vertical spinning motion to generate electricity. Both types of generators play a crucial role in harnessing the power of ocean currents for renewable energy production.</p></p>
