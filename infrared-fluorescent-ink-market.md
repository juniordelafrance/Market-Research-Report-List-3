<p><ul><li>Infrared Excited Ink</li><li>Infrared Absorption Ink</li><li>Infrared Covering Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infrared Fluorescent Ink market is categorized into three types: Infrared Excited Ink, Infrared Absorption Ink, and Infrared Covering Ink. Infrared Excited Ink is activated by an external infrared light source. Infrared Absorption Ink absorbs infrared light and emits a visible color. Infrared Covering Ink provides a protective layer against infrared light. Each type serves different purposes in anti-counterfeiting, security, and authentication applications, offering unique features for various industries such as banking, pharmaceuticals, and packaging.</p></p>
