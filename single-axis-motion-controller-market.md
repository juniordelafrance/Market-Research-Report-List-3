<p><p>Single-axis motion controllers are commonly available in two main types based on the type of motor used: stepper motor and servo motor. Controllers with stepper motors are popular for applications requiring low cost and simple operation, while controllers with servo motors are preferred for high precision and high-speed applications. Stepper motor controllers provide precise positioning by moving in discrete steps, while servo motor controllers offer smooth and accurate motion control through feedback mechanisms. Customers can choose the type that best suits their specific application requirements.</p></p>
