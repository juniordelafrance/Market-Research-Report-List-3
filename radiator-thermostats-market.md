<p><p>Radiator thermostats come in two main types - angled and straight. Angled radiator thermostats are designed to be installed on radiators that are positioned in corners or close to walls. The angled design allows for easier access to the temperature control knob. On the other hand, straight radiator thermostats are used for radiators that are placed in more open spaces or away from walls. Both types of thermostats serve the same purpose of regulating the temperature of radiators in a space-efficient manner.</p></p>
