<p><p>Account-Based Direct Mail Software is available in two main market types - On-Premises and Cloud-based. On-Premises software requires the software to be installed and operated on the host organization's servers, providing greater control but also requiring higher maintenance costs. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, operates on the vendor's servers and is accessed through the internet, offering easier scalability and lower upfront costs. Both types cater to different organizational needs, with On-Premises software being preferred by organizations with specific security requirements and Cloud-based software being popular for its flexibility and accessibility.</p></p>
