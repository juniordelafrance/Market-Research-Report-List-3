<p><p>The emerging inkjet printing market is divided into two main types: demand inkjet printing technology and continuous inkjet printing technology. Demand inkjet printing technology is characterized by its ability to quickly print high-quality images on a variety of surfaces, making it popular for small-scale and customizable printing projects. Continuous inkjet printing technology, on the other hand, is known for its ability to print on a continuous basis without the need for frequent ink cartridge changes, making it ideal for high-volume printing applications.</p></p>
