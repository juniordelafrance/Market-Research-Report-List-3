<p><p>IoT Telecom Services cater to various market types including Cellular, LPWAN (Low Power Wide Area Network), NB-IoT (Narrowband Internet of Things), and RF-Based markets. Cellular technology allows devices to connect through traditional mobile network towers. LPWAN enables long-range, low-power communication for IoT devices. NB-IoT is a cellular technology specifically designed for IoT applications requiring low power consumption. RF-Based technologies utilize radio frequencies for communication between devices. Each market type offers unique benefits and caters to different IoT needs.</p></p>
