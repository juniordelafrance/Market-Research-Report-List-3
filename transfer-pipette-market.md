<p><p>Transfer pipettes are commonly used in laboratories for the precise measurement and transfer of liquids. There are two main types of transfer pipettes: single-channel and multichannel. Single-channel transfer pipettes are designed for transferring one sample at a time, providing accurate measurements for individual samples. Multichannel transfer pipettes, on the other hand, are capable of transferring multiple samples simultaneously, making them ideal for high-throughput applications where efficiency and consistency are key. Both types of transfer pipettes play a crucial role in laboratory work, each offering unique benefits depending on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
