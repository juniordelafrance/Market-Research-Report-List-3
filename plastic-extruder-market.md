<p><strong>The Plastic Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Single-screw</li><li>Double-screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic extruders are commonly categorized into four main market types based on their design and functionality. Plunger extruders have a plunger that pushes the plastic material through a nozzle. Single-screw extruders use a single rotating screw to push the material through the machine. Double-screw extruders have two screws that work together to mix and push the material. Multi-screw extruders have more than two screws, allowing for greater control over the mixing and extrusion process. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited to different applications in the plastic industry.</p></p>
