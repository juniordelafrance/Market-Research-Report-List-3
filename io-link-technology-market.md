<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The IO-Link Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IO-Link Masters</li><li>IO-Link Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IO-Link Masters serve as the backbone of the IO-Link network, providing the interface between the control system and the IO-Link devices. They enable communication between the devices and the control system, allowing for real-time data exchange and parameterization. IO-Link Devices, on the other hand, are the sensors, actuators, and other devices that are connected to the IO-Link network. They provide valuable data to the control system, such as temperature, pressure, and position information, enhancing the overall functionality and efficiency of the system.</p></p>
