<p><p>Hydraulic surge brake actuators function as an essential component in braking systems for trailers and towed vehicles. The two main types of hydraulic surge brake actuators are disc brake surge actuators and drum brake surge actuators. Disc brake surge actuators use hydraulic pressure to engage the brakes through a disc brake system. On the other hand, drum brake surge actuators operate by transmitting hydraulic pressure to activate the brakes through a drum brake system. Both types play a crucial role in controlling the braking of trailers during towing.</p></p>
