<p><p>Tank Bottom Seat Valves are available in two main types: Manual Type and Pneumatic Type. Manual Type valves are operated by manual turning of a handle to open or close the valve, making them simple and cost-effective solutions for applications that do not require frequent changes in flow. Pneumatic Type valves, on the other hand, are operated by compressed air, offering automated control and precise regulation of flow rates. These valves are ideal for applications that require frequent adjustments or remote operation.</p></p>
