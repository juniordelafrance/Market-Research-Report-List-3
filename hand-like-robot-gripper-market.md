<p><p>Hand-like robot grippers come in three main market types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and servo-electric. Pneumatic grippers are powered by compressed air and are suitable for applications requiring fast and strong gripping. Hydraulic grippers use fluid power to provide precise force and speed control, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Servo-electric grippers utilize electric motors for precise and programmable gripping, making them versatile and easy to integrate into automated systems. Each gripper type has unique advantages and is suited to different applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
