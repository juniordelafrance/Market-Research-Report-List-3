<p><strong>The LED Light Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED Arrays</li><li>Single LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Light Module Market consists of two main types, LED Arrays, and Single LEDs. LED Arrays contain multiple LED chips assembled together on a single module, providing higher brightness and efficiency. They are often used in applications where strong and uniform lighting is required. On the other hand, Single LEDs are individual LED chips mounted on a module, offering flexibility in design and versatile use. They are commonly used in smaller lighting fixtures or displays that require precise and focused illumination.</p></p>
