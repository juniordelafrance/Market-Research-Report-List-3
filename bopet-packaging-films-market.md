<p><p>BOPET packaging films are categorized based on their thickness into four main types. Films with thickness below 15 μm are generally used for applications requiring high transparency and good tensile strength. Films with thickness between 15-30 μm are commonly used for flexible packaging solutions. Films falling in the range of 30-50 μm are ideal for applications requiring high barrier properties and durability. Films with thickness above 50 μm are suitable for heavy-duty packaging applications that require excellent strength and protection.</p></p>
