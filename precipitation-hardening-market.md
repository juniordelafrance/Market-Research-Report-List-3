<p><strong>The Precipitation Hardening Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coherency Strain Precipitation Hardening</li><li>Chemical Precipitation Hardening</li><li>Dispersion Precipitation Hardening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coherency Strain Precipitation Hardening involves the formation of fine particles within a crystal structure due to strain caused by the difference in size between the precipitate and the crystal lattice. Chemical Precipitation Hardening occurs when a chemical reaction leads to the formation of fine particles that strengthen the material. Dispersion Precipitation Hardening involves uniformly dispersing fine particles within a material to increase its strength. These different types of precipitation hardening methods all aim to improve the mechanical properties of materials through the formation of precipitates.</p></p>
