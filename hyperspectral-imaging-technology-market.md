<p><p>Hyperspectral imaging technology market consists of two main types: Spatial scanning technology and Spectral scanning technology. Spatial scanning technology involves capturing images of an object or scene in various spatial locations to create a detailed spatial map. On the other hand, Spectral scanning technology involves capturing images of an object or scene at various wavelengths of light to gather detailed spectral information. Both types of technology play a crucial role in various industries such as agriculture, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
