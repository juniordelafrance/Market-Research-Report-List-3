<p><strong>The Fisheries Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-site Treatment</li><li>Off-site Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fisheries management market can be categorized into on-site treatment and off-site treatment. On-site treatment involves the processing of fish at the same location where they are caught, reducing transportation costs and ensuring freshness. Off-site treatment, on the other hand, involves transporting fish to a separate facility for processing, which can result in higher costs but allows for greater scale of operations and specialization. Both market types play a critical role in ensuring sustainable and efficient utilization of aquatic resources.</p></p>
