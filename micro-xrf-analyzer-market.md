<p><p>Micro-XRF Analyzer market is divided into two main types: Benchtop and Portable. Benchtop analyzers are stationary instruments that are typically used in laboratory settings for high-precision analysis. They offer higher sensitivity and resolution but are not portable. On the other hand, Portable analyzers are compact, lightweight instruments that can be easily transported to various locations for on-site analysis. They are often used in fields such as archaeology, geology, and environmental science where samples cannot be easily transported to a lab.</p></p>
