<p><p>Emergency beds come in three main types: electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric beds are powered by electricity and can be adjusted with the push of a button, making it easier for caregivers and patients to adjust the bed position. Manual beds require physical effort to adjust, usually by turning a crank or lever. Hydraulic beds use hydraulic systems to make adjustments, providing a smoother and quieter operation compared to manual beds. Each type of bed caters to different needs and preferences in emergency care settings.</p></p>
