<p><p>Particle size distribution measurement instruments market can be categorized into Laser Diffraction, Dynamic Light Scattering, Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis, and Others. Laser Diffraction instruments determine particle size by measuring how light scatters as it passes through a sample. Dynamic Light Scattering instruments focus on the Brownian motion of particles in solution to determine size. Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis observes individual particles in motion to calculate size distribution. Other instruments may include imaging techniques or sieve analysis for particle size measurement.</p></p>
