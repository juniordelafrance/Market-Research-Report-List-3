<p><p>Sleep Apnea Treatment Devices market includes various types such as Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Devices, Oxygen Devices, Oral Appliances, and Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) Devices. Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Devices are the most common treatment option, providing continuous air pressure to keep airways open during sleep. Oxygen Devices deliver supplemental oxygen to improve breathing. Oral Appliances are custom-fit devices worn in the mouth to reposition the jaw and tongue. Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV) Devices provide pressure support based on individual breathing patterns.</p></p>
