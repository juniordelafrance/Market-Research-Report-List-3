<p><ul><li>UVA</li><li>UVB</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) sensors can be categorized into three main types: UVA, UVB, and Others. UVA sensors specifically detect long-wave UVA rays, which account for the majority of UV radiation reaching the Earth's surface. UVB sensors, on the other hand, are designed to detect medium-wave UVB rays, which are known to cause sunburn and skin damage. The category of Others includes sensors that may detect a combination of UVA and UVB rays or other specific forms of UV radiation.</p></p>
