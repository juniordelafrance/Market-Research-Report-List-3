<p><ul><li>Silicon Free Defoamer</li><li>Silica Defoamer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cement defoamers primarily consist of two types: Silicone free defoamers and Silica defoamers. Silicone free defoamers are formulated without any silicone components, making them ideal for applications where silicone use is restricted. On the other hand, Silica defoamers contain silica compounds that help in reducing foam formation in cement mixtures. Both types of defoamers are efficient in controlling foam levels in cement production processes, providing enhanced performance and improved quality of cement products.</p></p>
