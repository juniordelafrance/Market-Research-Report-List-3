Porcine Gelatin is derived from pigs, while Bovine Gelatin is derived from cows. Both types work by promoting the formation of blood clots to stop bleeding. Healthcare professionals may choose one type over the other based on factors such as patient allergies or personal preferences. Both types are effective in providing hemostasis and are widely used in medical settings.</p></p>
