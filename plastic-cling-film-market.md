<p><p>Plastic cling film is available in various types such as PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), and PMP (polyether-block-amide). PE cling film is commonly used for household food wrapping, while PVC cling film is known for its superior cling and strength. PVDC cling film offers excellent barrier properties to moisture and gases. PMP cling film is a newer type made from a blend of materials providing enhanced strength and tear resistance, making it suitable for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
