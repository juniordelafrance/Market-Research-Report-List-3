<p><p>Mini C-Arms are used for imaging in orthopedic, podiatric, and emergency room procedures. There are two types of Mini C-Arms available in the market: Mini C-Arm with Pulsed Fluoroscopy and Mini C-Arm with Continuous Fluoroscopy. Mini C-Arm with Pulsed Fluoroscopy uses short bursts of radiation to capture images, reducing overall radiation exposure. Mini C-Arm with Continuous Fluoroscopy provides real-time imaging capabilities, allowing for continuous visualization during procedures. Both types offer advanced imaging solutions tailored to specific medical needs.</p></p>
