<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Full-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic laser welding systems can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic systems require some level of human intervention during the welding process, such as loading and unloading parts or adjusting parameters. On the other hand, full-automatic systems operate without the need for manual intervention, utilizing advanced technologies like robotics and sensor systems to carry out the entire welding process autonomously. Both types have their own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the welding operation.</p></p>
