<p><p>Wall hung combination boilers can be categorized into two types: condensing and non-condensing. Condensing boilers are more energy efficient as they recycle heat from flue gases, resulting in lower energy consumption and reduced emissions. Non-condensing boilers, on the other hand, do not recycle heat and are less efficient. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as budget, energy efficiency goals, and environmental impact considerations. Both types have their own unique advantages and are suitable for different needs in the market.</p></p>
