<p><p>The optical network component and subsystem market consist of three main types: active components, passive components, and optical modules. Active components, like lasers and modulators, actively manipulate signals in the network. Passive components, such as filters and splitters, simply transmit signals without needing power. Optical modules combine active and passive components to perform specific functions within the network. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall efficiency and performance of optical networks.</p></p>
