<p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) Systems are designed to completely eliminate liquid waste discharge, making them crucial in industries facing strict environmental regulations. There are two main types of ZLD systems: Conventional and Hybrid. Conventional ZLD systems utilize multiple processes such as evaporation, crystallization, and reverse osmosis to achieve zero liquid discharge. Hybrid ZLD systems combine conventional methods with innovative technologies like forward osmosis and membrane distillation to optimize water reuse and minimize waste generation. Both types play a vital role in sustainable water management practices.</p></p>
