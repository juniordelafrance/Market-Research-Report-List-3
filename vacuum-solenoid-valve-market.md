They are commonly used in applications where immediate on/off control is necessary. On the other hand, Semi-Direct Acting Vacuum Solenoid Valves utilize a pilot valve to control the flow of vacuum, allowing for a wider range of pressure control. These valves are often used in applications where variable pressure levels are required for optimal performance.</p></p>
