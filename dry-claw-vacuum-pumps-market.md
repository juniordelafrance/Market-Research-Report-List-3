<p><p>Dry claw vacuum pumps are classified into two main categories: single-stage dry claw vacuum pumps and multi-stage dry claw vacuum pumps. Single-stage pumps utilize a single set of claw rotors to create vacuum pressure, making them suitable for applications with relatively low vacuum requirements. On the other hand, multi-stage pumps use multiple sets of claw rotors in series to achieve higher levels of vacuum pressure, making them ideal for demanding industrial applications that require deeper vacuum levels.</p></p>
