<p><p>SMD Potentiometers are available in two main types: Digital and Analog. Digital potentiometers use digital signals to adjust resistance, allowing for precise and programmable control. They are commonly used in applications where accuracy is crucial, such as in audio equipment and automotive systems. Analog potentiometers, on the other hand, use analog signals to adjust resistance, providing smooth and continuous control. They are commonly used in applications where a simple and cost-effective solution is required, such as in lighting dimmers and volume controls.</p></p>
