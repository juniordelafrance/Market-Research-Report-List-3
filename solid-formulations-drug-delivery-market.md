<p><p>Solid formulations drug delivery market includes powders and suppositories. Powders are a type of solid dosage form that consists of finely ground particles of active pharmaceutical ingredients. They are often mixed with excipients to improve flow and stability. Suppositories are solid dosage forms that are inserted into the rectum or vagina for localized drug delivery. They are typically made of a combination of active pharmaceutical ingredients and various bases, such as cocoa butter or gelatin.</p></p>
