<p><p>Plant growth chambers come in two main types: reach-in and walk-in. Reach-in chambers are ideal for smaller scale experiments or studies, providing easy access to the plants inside. They are commonly used in research labs or educational settings. Walk-in chambers, on the other hand, are larger and allow for more plants to be grown at once. They are often used for commercial purposes or large-scale research projects. Both types of chambers provide controlled environments for optimal plant growth and research.</p></p>
