<p><p>Air suspension valve blocks are essential components in air suspension systems, regulating air flow to individual suspension corners. For 2-corner air suspension systems, valve blocks control air pressure and delivery to two suspension corners independently, allowing for customized ride height and comfort. On the other hand, for 4-corner air suspension systems, valve blocks manage air distribution to all four suspension corners, ensuring balanced performance and stability. These different types cater to varying suspension requirements and preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
