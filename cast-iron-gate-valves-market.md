<p><p>Cast iron gate valves with flanged connection are commonly used in industrial applications where a sturdy and leak-proof connection is required. These valves are connected to pipes through flanges using bolts and are suitable for high-pressure systems. On the other hand, cast iron gate valves with threaded connection are more convenient for residential and small-scale applications. They can be easily installed without the need for welding and are ideal for low to medium pressure systems. Both types offer reliable control of fluids in pipelines.</p></p>
