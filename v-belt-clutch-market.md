<p><p>V-Belt Clutch Market offers two main types: Dry Type and Wet Type clutches. Dry type clutches use friction to transfer power, relying on the contact between the belt and pulley surfaces. This type is commonly seen in applications where oil or lubricants are not suitable. On the other hand, Wet type clutches use oil or other lubricants to reduce friction and transfer power more efficiently. This type is preferred in applications where cooling and smooth engagement are crucial. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
