<p><p>Powder molding hydraulic press machines are mainly categorized into three types - frame hydraulic press, four-column hydraulic press, and others. Frame hydraulic press machines are designed with a solid frame structure for better stability and rigidity. Four-column hydraulic press machines have four vertical columns to provide better precision and uniform pressure distribution during the molding process. Other types of hydraulic press machines include C-frame, H-frame, and custom-designed presses that cater to specific requirements of different industries.</p></p>
