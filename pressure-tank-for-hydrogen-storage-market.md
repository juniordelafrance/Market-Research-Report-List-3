<p><p>Pressure tanks for hydrogen storage are categorized into four types based on the material used for construction and suitability for storage pressure - Type I, Type II, Type III, and Type IV. Type I tanks are made of metal and are heavy but offer high durability. Type II tanks use a metal liner wrapped with a composite material for lighter weight. Type III tanks have a fully composite structure for reduced weight and improved safety. Type IV tanks have a plastic liner reinforced with composite materials for the lightest weight and highest safety.</p></p>
