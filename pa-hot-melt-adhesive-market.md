<p><p>PA hot melt adhesive is commonly available in two forms in the market: powder and granules. Granular PA hot melt adhesive consists of solid pellets that are heated to a molten state before application. Both forms offer excellent bonding properties and are commonly used in various industries such as packaging, automotive, and construction for their fast-setting and strong adhesion capabilities.</p></p>
