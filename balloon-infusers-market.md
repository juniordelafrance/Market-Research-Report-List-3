<p><p>Continuous rate pumps in the balloon infusers market are devices that deliver a constant flow of medication through the balloon catheter at a steady rate. This type of pump is useful for patients requiring a consistent medication dosage over a period of time. On the other hand, variable rate pumps provide the flexibility to adjust the medication delivery rate according to the patient's changing needs. This type of pump is beneficial for patients who require different medication dosages at different times.</p></p>
