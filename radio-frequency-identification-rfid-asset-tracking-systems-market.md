<p><p>Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Asset Tracking Systems come in two main types - Active RFID and Passive RFID. Active RFID systems use battery-powered tags that continuously transmit data to a reader, allowing for real-time tracking over larger distances. Passive RFID systems, on the other hand, rely on tags that are powered by the reader's signal, making them less expensive and suitable for shorter-range applications. Both types of systems offer unique benefits and cater to different tracking needs in various industries.</p></p>
