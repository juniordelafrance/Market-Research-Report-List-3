<p><p>Stainless Steel Gate Valves come in two main market types: Flanged Connection and Threaded Connection. Flanged Connection gate valves are installed using flanges, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Threaded Connection gate valves are installed using threaded fittings, making them easy to install and ideal for situations where welding is not desired. Both types offer corrosion resistance, durability, and reliability, making them popular choices in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
