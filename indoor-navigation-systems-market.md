<p><p>Indoor navigation systems utilize various technologies, including Ultra-Wideband (UWB), Wi-Fi, Bluetooth Low Energy, Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID), and others to provide accurate indoor positioning and navigation services. UWB technology offers high precision location tracking, while Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Low Energy are commonly used for indoor positioning in smartphones and other devices. RFID systems use electromagnetic fields to locate and track tags attached to objects. Other technologies, such as infrared and magnetic positioning, also play a role in indoor navigation systems.</p></p>
