<p><p>Single-stage snowblowers are typically smaller and more lightweight, making them easier to maneuver. They are ideal for light to moderate snowfall and can clear snow down to the pavement. Two-stage snowblowers are larger and more powerful, designed for heavy snowfall and larger areas. They have an auger to gather and break up the snow, then a impeller to throw it out of the chute. Two-stage snowblowers are perfect for clearing large driveways and walkways efficiently.</p></p>
