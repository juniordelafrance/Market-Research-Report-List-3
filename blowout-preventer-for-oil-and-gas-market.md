<p><p>A Blowout Preventer (BOP) is a safety device used in oil and gas drilling operations to prevent the uncontrolled release of oil and gas from a well. There are two main types of BOPs: Annular Blowout Preventers and Ram Blowout Preventers. Annular BOPs have a flexible rubber seal that can close around any size of drill pipe, while Ram BOPs have two or more hydraulically-operated rams that can seal off the wellbore. Both types of BOPs play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of drilling operations in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
