<p><p>The Ultra-Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device market includes OCT Camera and Fundus Camera market types. OCT Camera uses light waves to take cross-section pictures of the retina, providing detailed images of the layers of the eye. Fundus Camera, on the other hand, captures images of the back of the eye, including the retina and optic nerve. Both technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions, making them essential tools for ophthalmologists and optometrists.</p></p>
