<p><p>Isolated Open Loop Current Sensors are designed to provide electrical isolation between the current carrying conductor and the sensing element, ensuring safety and protection in high voltage applications. Non-isolated Open Loop Current Sensors do not have this isolation feature, making them suitable for low voltage applications where isolation is not necessary. The choice between isolated and non-isolated sensors depends on the specific requirements of the application, such as level of voltage, safety considerations, and cost constraints.</p></p>
