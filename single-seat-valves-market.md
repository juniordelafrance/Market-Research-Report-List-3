<p><p>Single seat valves are commonly used in industries for controlling the flow of fluids. There are two main types of single seat valves based on their actuation methods: manual valves and pneumatic valves. Manual valves are operated by hand, allowing for precise control over the flow rate. On the other hand, pneumatic valves are operated by air pressure, making them suitable for automated applications where quick and reliable actuation is required. Both types of valves serve the same purpose of regulating fluid flow but differ in their method of operation.</p></p>
