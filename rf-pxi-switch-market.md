<p><strong>The RF PXI Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SPDT switch</li><li>Double pole double throw switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF PXI Switch market consists of SPDT switches and Double pole double throw switches. SPDT switches have a single input and output, allowing for the selection between two paths. They are commonly used in RF applications for signal routing. Double pole double throw switches have two inputs and two outputs, enabling the selection between two sets of connections simultaneously. They are suitable for more complex RF switching operations where multiple paths need to be managed.</p></p>
