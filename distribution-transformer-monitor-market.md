<p><ul><li>Single-phase Distribution Transformer Monitor</li><li>Poly-phase Distribution Transformer Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-phase Distribution Transformer Monitors are designed for single-phase distribution transformers and are ideal for residential and small commercial applications. They provide real-time monitoring of transformer parameters such as temperature, load, and power quality.</p><p>Poly-phase Distribution Transformer Monitors, on the other hand, are suitable for larger, three-phase distribution transformers used in industrial and utility applications. These monitors offer advanced sensing capabilities for measuring parameters across multiple phases, providing more detailed insights into transformer performance and efficiency. Both types of monitors help improve transformer reliability, prevent outages, and optimize maintenance schedules.</p></p>
