<p><p>Oilfield exploration market consists of four main types: Geological method involves studying rock formations and structures to determine potential oil reservoirs. Geophysical method involves using seismic surveys and other techniques to identify underground oil deposits. Geochemical method analyzes rock and fluid samples for signs of oil presence. Drilling method involves physically extracting oil from the ground. Each of these methods plays a crucial role in discovering and extracting oil reserves, contributing to the overall success of the oilfield exploration market.</p></p>
