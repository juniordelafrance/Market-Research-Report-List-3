<p><p>Dental composite fillings are a popular choice for restoring teeth due to their aesthetic appeal and durability. There are different types of dental composite fillings, with resin-based composites being the most commonly used. These fillings are made from a mixture of resin and small glass or ceramic particles. Another type is poly-acid modified composites, which contain a small amount of acid to improve bond strength with the tooth structure. Both types of composite fillings offer excellent esthetics and durability, making them a preferred choice for many dental patients.</p></p>
