<p><p>Ceramic screws are categorized into two main types: Alumina Screws and Zirconia Screws. Alumina screws are made from aluminum oxide and are known for their high strength and resistance to wear and corrosion, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, Zirconia screws are made from zirconium dioxide and are prized for their exceptional toughness and biocompatibility, making them ideal for use in medical and dental implants. Both types of ceramic screws offer superior performance compared to traditional metal screws.</p></p>
