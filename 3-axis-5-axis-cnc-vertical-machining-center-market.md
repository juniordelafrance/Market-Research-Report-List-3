<p><p>3 axis, 4 axis, and 5 axis CNC Vertical Machining Centers are differentiated by the number of axes they can move along. A 3 axis machine can move along the X, Y, and Z axes, suitable for basic machining tasks. A 4 axis machine adds a rotary axis, enabling tilt movement for more complex designs. A 5 axis machine further adds a swivel rotary axis, allowing for intricate and multi-sided machining, making it ideal for high precision and complex parts manufacturing.</p></p>
