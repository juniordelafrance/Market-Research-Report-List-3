<p><strong>The Automotive IGBT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIB/PIM</li><li>IPM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive IGBT Market can be divided into two types: CIB/PIM and IPM. CIB/PIM stands for Converter Inverter Brake/ Power Integrated Module, which integrates multiple functions into a single unit, increasing efficiency and reducing overall system complexity. IPM, on the other hand, stands for Intelligent Power Module, which features advanced power management capabilities and control functions. Both types of IGBT technologies play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of automotive systems, catering to the increasing demand for electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
