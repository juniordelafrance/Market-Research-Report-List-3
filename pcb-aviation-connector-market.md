Circular Type connectors are commonly used in aviation applications due to their secure locking mechanism and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Rectangular Type connectors are mainly used for their space-saving design and high contact density, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. Both types of connectors play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and efficient communication and power transmission in aviation systems.</p></p>
