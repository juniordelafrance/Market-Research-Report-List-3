<p><p>Ballistic separators are used in waste management to separate materials based on their shape and density. Single-stage ballistic separators utilize paddles or discs to propel materials of different shapes into separate streams. Double-stage ballistic separators further refine this process by using additional paddles or discs to further segregate materials based on their size and density. Both types of ballistic separators are effective in improving recycling processes by efficiently sorting materials for more effective processing and recycling.</p></p>
