<p><p>Underwater plasma cutting machines can be categorized into two main types: CNC underwater plasma cutting machines and inverter underwater plasma cutting machines. CNC underwater plasma cutting machines are controlled by computer numerical control systems, allowing for precise and automated cutting operations. Inverter underwater plasma cutting machines use inverter technology to control the electrical output, resulting in efficient and high-quality cuts. Both types of machines are commonly used in underwater cutting applications for industries such as shipbuilding, oil & gas, and underwater construction.</p></p>
