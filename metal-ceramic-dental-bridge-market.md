<p><p>Metal ceramic dental bridges are a popular option for replacing missing teeth. There are several types of metal ceramic dental bridges available on the market. Traditional bridges involve creating a crown for the tooth or implant on either side of the missing tooth, with a pontic in between. Cantilever bridges are used when there are only teeth on one side of the missing teeth. Maryland bridges use metal or porcelain wings bonded to existing teeth. Implant-supported bridges are attached to dental implants for a more secure fit.</p></p>
