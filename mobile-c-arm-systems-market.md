<p><p>Mobile C-arm systems can be classified into two main types: Little C-arms and Peripheral interventional C-arm systems. Little C-arms are smaller and more portable devices, commonly used in orthopedic and trauma surgeries. On the other hand, Peripheral interventional C-arms are larger units designed for more complex procedures such as vascular and cardiac interventions. Both types of C-arm systems play a crucial role in providing real-time imaging during minimally invasive surgeries, improving accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
