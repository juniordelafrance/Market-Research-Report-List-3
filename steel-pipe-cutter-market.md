<p><p>Steel pipe cutters are available in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual pipe cutters require physical effort to operate, with the user rotating a handle to cut through the pipe. These are best for smaller projects or when a precise cut is needed. On the other hand, automatic pipe cutters are motorized and require minimal effort to operate, making them ideal for larger projects or continuous use. Both types of pipe cutters are widely used in construction, plumbing, and other industries for cutting steel pipes efficiently and accurately.</p></p>
