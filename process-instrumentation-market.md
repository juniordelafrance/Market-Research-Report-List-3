<p><p>Process instrumentation market consists of three main types: control valve, field instrument, and analyzer. Control valves are used to regulate the flow of liquids or gases in industrial processes. Field instruments measure and monitor various process parameters such as temperature, pressure, level, and flow. Analyzers are used to analyze the composition of gases, liquids, or solids in a process. These types of instrumentation are essential for ensuring that industrial processes run smoothly, efficiently, and safely.</p></p>
