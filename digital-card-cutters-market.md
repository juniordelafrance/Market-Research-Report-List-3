<p><p>Digital card cutters come in two main types: blade cutting and laser cutting. Blade cutting uses a sharp blade to precisely cut through the material, while laser cutting uses a high-powered laser to vaporize the material. Blade cutting is cost-effective and suitable for more basic designs, while laser cutting offers more intricate and precise cuts. Both types are popular in the market for their speed, accuracy, and ability to handle a variety of materials.</p></p>
