<p><p>Pneumatic ISO cylinders can be categorized into three main types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and others. Single-acting cylinders use air pressure to move in one direction, with a spring return mechanism for the opposite direction. Double-acting cylinders utilize air pressure to move in both directions, providing more control and flexibility in various applications. The "others" category includes specialized cylinders such as tandem cylinders, rotary cylinders, and rodless cylinders, each designed for specific requirements in industrial processes.</p></p>
