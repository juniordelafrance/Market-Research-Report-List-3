<p><p>Boom lifts are aerial work platforms used for reaching heights in various industries. Telescopic boom lifts extend straight out and up, ideal for vertical reach. Articulating boom lifts have multiple joints for reaching up and over obstacles. Trailer-mounted boom lifts are mobile and easy to transport. Bucket trucks have an extendable arm with a bucket for workers to stand in. Towable boom lifts are compact and can be towed behind a vehicle. Each type offers unique advantages for different job requirements in the market.</p></p>
