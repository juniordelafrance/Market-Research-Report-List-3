<p><p>Standard treadmill market consists of two main types: manual treadmills and motorised treadmills. Manual treadmills are powered by the user's movement while walking or running on the machine, offering a more budget-friendly option. Motorised treadmills, on the other hand, are equipped with a motor that controls the speed and incline electronically, providing a smoother and more customizable workout experience.
