<p><p>Stone extractors come in three main types based on their operations: Manual, Automatic, and Semi-Automatic. Manual stone extractors require human intervention for every step of the extraction process, making them labor-intensive but cost-effective. The Automatic stone extractors are fully automated, requiring minimal human involvement and increasing efficiency. The Semi-Automatic stone extractors strike a balance between the two, offering some level of automation while still allowing for manual control when needed. Each type of stone extractor caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
