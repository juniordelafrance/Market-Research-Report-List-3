<p><strong>The Wafer Handling Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmosheric Transfer Robot</li><li>Vacuum Transfer Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer handling robots are categorized into two main types: atmospheric transfer robots and vacuum transfer robots. Atmospheric transfer robots are designed to handle wafers in ambient air environments, using mechanical arms and gripping tools to transfer wafers between various processing tools. On the other hand, vacuum transfer robots utilize vacuum technology to securely hold and move wafers in a controlled, low-pressure environment, ensuring minimal particle contamination and damage to the wafers during handling and transfer processes. Each type of robot caters to specific requirements in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
