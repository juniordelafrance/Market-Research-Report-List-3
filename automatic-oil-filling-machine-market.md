<p><p>Automatic oil filling machines come in two main types: adjustable height and fixed height. Adjustable height machines allow for flexibility in filling different containers of varying sizes. They can easily accommodate containers of different heights by adjusting the position of the filling nozzle. On the other hand, fixed height machines have a stationary filling nozzle that is set at a specific height. This type of machine is suitable for filling a standard size container consistently without the need for frequent adjustments.</p></p>
