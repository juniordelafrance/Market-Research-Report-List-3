<p><p>Nickel Matte Market Types refer to different processes used for the extraction of nickel from its ore. Layered smelting involves heating the ore with fluxes to separate the nickel from other elements. Leaching involves dissolving the nickel from the ore with a chemical solution. The Carbonyl Process involves the conversion of nickel oxides into nickel carbonyl gas, which is then decomposed to form pure nickel. The Flotation Process involves separating nickel minerals from other materials using air bubbles. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the nickel industry.</p></p>
