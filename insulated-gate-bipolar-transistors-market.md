Discrete IGBTs are single standalone devices used in various applications including power supplies and motor control. On the other hand, IGBT modules are combinations of multiple IGBTs along with other necessary components such as diodes and heat sink, providing higher power handling capabilities and efficiency. Both types cater to different market needs and are crucial components in modern power electronics systems.</p></p>
