<p><p>Infrared (IR) sensors can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensors and Quantum Infrared (QIR) sensors. TIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted from objects based on their temperature, while QIR sensors rely on quantum mechanics principles for detection. TIR sensors are commonly used in applications such as temperature monitoring and night vision, while QIR sensors are used in areas like remote sensing and communication. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for detecting and measuring infrared radiation.</p></p>
