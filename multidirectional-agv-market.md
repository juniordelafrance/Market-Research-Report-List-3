<p><p>Multidirectional AGVs in the market are guided by either Optical Guiding or Magnetic Guiding technologies. Optical Guiding AGVs use cameras or sensors to follow predefined paths marked with visual cues, allowing for flexibility and adaptability in changing environments. Magnetic Guiding AGVs follow magnetic strips or markers embedded in the floor, providing precise and reliable navigation. Both technologies offer efficient and accurate guidance for AGVs in various industries, helping to streamline material handling processes and improve overall productivity.</p></p>
