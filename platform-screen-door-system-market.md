<p><ul><li>Full Height</li><li>Half Height</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Platform Screen Door System market is divided into two main types: Full Height and Half Height. Full Height systems extend from floor to ceiling, providing a complete barrier between the platform and tracks. They offer maximum protection and security. Half Height systems are shorter, reaching up to about waist or chest height. They provide some level of protection but do not completely block off the platform. The choice between full height and half height systems depends on factors such as safety requirements, space limitations, and budget constraints.</p></p>
