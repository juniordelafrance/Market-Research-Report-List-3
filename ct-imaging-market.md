<p><p>CT imaging market comprises X-ray CT, Positron Emission Tomography (PET), and Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). X-ray CT uses X-rays to create detailed images of internal body structures. PET imaging involves injecting a radioactive tracer into the body to detect diseases like cancer and brain disorders. SPECT imaging also uses radioactive tracers to create 3D images of blood flow in various organs. These imaging technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring a wide range of medical conditions.</p></p>
