<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Split Septum Needleless Connector market can be classified into three types based on fluid displacement mechanism: Positive Fluid Displacement, Negative Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. In Positive Fluid Displacement connectors, fluid is pushed out of the connector when accessing the system. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors prevent backflow of blood into the device. Neutral Displacement connectors maintain a neutral pressure environment without any fluid movement during access. Each type offers unique benefits and is designed to meet specific clinical needs.</p></p>
