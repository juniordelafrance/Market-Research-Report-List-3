<p><p>Nuclear waste recycling market involves various methods for managing and disposing of radioactive waste. Direct disposal methods focus on burying waste deep underground in specially designed repositories. Underwater storage involves placing waste in water-filled pools or tanks to shield radiation. Nuclear waste vitrification transforms waste into glass for long-term storage. Other markets include reprocessing and reuse of spent nuclear fuel, or converting waste into new fuel. Each method aims to safely contain and reduce the volume of radioactive waste.</p></p>
