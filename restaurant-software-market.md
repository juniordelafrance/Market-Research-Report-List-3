<p><strong>The Restaurant Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional on-Premise</li><li>Cloud (SaaS Delivery Model)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional on-Premise restaurant software refers to systems that are installed and operated on-site at a restaurant's location. This type of software requires the restaurant to purchase and maintain physical servers and infrastructure. On the other hand, Cloud (SaaS Delivery Model) restaurant software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This type of software is typically subscription-based and allows for easier scalability and flexibility. Cloud software also offers features like real-time data access and automatic updates, making it a popular choice for modern restaurants.</p></p>
