<p><p>Coating machines can be classified into three types based on their operation modes: Automatic, Semi-automatic, and Manual. Automatic coating machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. Semi-automatic machines involve some degree of manual operation along with automated processes. Manual coating machines require complete manual operation by the operator. Each type caters to different production needs and levels of efficiency. Automatic machines are suitable for high-volume production, semi-automatic machines offer a balance of automation and manual control, while manual machines are ideal for small-scale productions or customized operations.</p></p>
