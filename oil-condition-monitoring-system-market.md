<p><p>Oil Condition Monitoring System Market involves both on-site and off-site types. On-site monitoring involves real-time analysis of oil samples at the location where the equipment is in use. This type of monitoring provides immediate feedback on the condition of the oil and the equipment. Off-site monitoring, on the other hand, involves sending oil samples to a testing facility for analysis. This type of monitoring provides a comprehensive analysis of the oil's condition and can identify potential issues before they become critical. Both types of monitoring are essential for maintaining the efficiency and longevity of equipment.</p></p>
