<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic gate operators are widely used in residential, commercial, and industrial settings to automate the movement of gates for security and convenience. There are two main types of automatic gate operators: mechanical and hydraulic. </p><p>Mechanical gate operators use gears and chains to operate the gate, while hydraulic gate operators utilize hydraulic fluid to power the gate movement. Mechanical gate operators are generally more cost-effective and easier to install, while hydraulic gate operators are preferred for heavier and larger gates due to their increased power and reliability.</p></p>
