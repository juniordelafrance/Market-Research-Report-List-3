<p><strong>The Edge Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Pass Wave</li><li>Short Pass Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Edge filters are optical devices that transmit light within a certain wavelength range while blocking light outside that range. Long pass wave filters allow longer wavelengths to pass through while blocking shorter wavelengths. These filters are commonly used in applications where only specific wavelengths of light are needed, such as in fluorescence microscopy. Short pass wave filters, on the other hand, transmit shorter wavelengths while blocking longer wavelengths. They are used in applications such as Raman spectroscopy where shorter wavelengths are required for analysis.</p></p>
