<p><strong>The Engine Exhaust System Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Exhaust Muffler</li><li>Reactive Exhaust Muffler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine exhaust system muffler market can be divided into two main types: absorption exhaust mufflers and reactive exhaust mufflers. Absorption mufflers work by using sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise levels, while reactive mufflers use resonant chambers to cancel out specific sound frequencies. Absorption mufflers are more commonly used for everyday vehicles, while reactive mufflers are often found in high-performance vehicles where precise tuning of exhaust noise is desired. Both types play a crucial role in reducing noise pollution and improving overall driving experience.</p></p>
