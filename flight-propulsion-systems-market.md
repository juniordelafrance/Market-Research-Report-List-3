<p><p>Flight propulsion systems market can be divided into two main types: Air Breathing Engines and Non-Air Breathing Engines. Air Breathing Engines rely on taking in oxygen from the atmosphere for fuel combustion, while Non-Air Breathing Engines carry their own oxidizer, such as solid rocket engines. Air Breathing Engines are more common in commercial aircraft as they are more fuel-efficient, while Non-Air Breathing Engines are typically used in military applications where greater power is required. Both types play a crucial role in the aviation industry.</p></p>
