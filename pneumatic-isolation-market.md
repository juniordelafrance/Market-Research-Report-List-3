<p><ul><li>Push Pneumatic Isolation</li><li>Pull Pneumatic Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic isolation systems are used to separate different areas within a facility to prevent contamination, control air flow, and maintain desired pressure levels. Push pneumatic isolation systems utilize positive air pressure to push contaminants away from the isolated area, while pull pneumatic isolation systems use negative air pressure to pull contaminants away. Both types of systems are crucial in maintaining clean environments in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics manufacturing, and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
