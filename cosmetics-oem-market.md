<p><p>In the cosmetics OEM market, there are two main types of markets: All process OEM and Half process OEM. In the All process OEM market, the manufacturer is responsible for all stages of production, from formulation to packaging. On the other hand, the Half process OEM market involves manufacturers handling only a portion of the production process, such as formulation or packaging. Companies may choose between these market types based on their specific needs, capabilities, and resources.</p></p>
