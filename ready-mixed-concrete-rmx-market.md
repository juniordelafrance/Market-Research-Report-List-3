<p><p>Ready Mixed Concrete (RMX) is a popular type of concrete that is produced in a batching plant and then transported to the construction site where it is used. There are three main types of RMX markets. Transit Mixed Concrete involves mixing the concrete en route to the construction site. Shrink Mixed Concrete involves partially mixing the concrete at the plant and completing the mixing process at the construction site. Central Mixed Concrete involves completely mixing the concrete at the plant before transporting it to the site.</p></p>
