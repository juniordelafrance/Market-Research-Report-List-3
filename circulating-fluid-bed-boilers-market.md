<p><p>Circulating Fluidized Bed Boilers are classified into three main types based on the operating pressure and temperature: Subcritical, Supercritical, and Ultra-supercritical. Subcritical boilers operate below the critical point of water, Supercritical boilers operate above the critical point resulting in improved efficiency, while Ultra-supercritical boilers operate at even higher temperatures and pressures for enhanced efficiency and reduced emissions. Each type offers different advantages in terms of energy efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact.</p></p>
