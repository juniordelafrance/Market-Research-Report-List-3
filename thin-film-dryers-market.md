<p><p>Thin Film Dryers are typically classified into two main types based on their orientation: Vertical Thin Film Dryers and Horizontal Thin Film Dryers. Vertical Thin Film Dryers are designed with the heating surface located on the inside of the vertical cylindrical vessel, allowing for efficient heat transfer and evaporation of moisture from the product. Horizontal Thin Film Dryers, on the other hand, have a horizontal orientation with the heating surface on the exterior, allowing for continuous drying of the product as it moves through the dryer.</p></p>
