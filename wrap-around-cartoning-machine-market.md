<p><p>Wrap-around cartoning machines come in two main market types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as loading the products into the machine before it seals the carton. This type is ideal for smaller production volumes. On the other hand, automatic machines are fully automated, performing all functions from product loading to carton sealing without the need for manual assistance. These machines are typically used in high-volume production settings where efficiency and speed are crucial.</p></p>
