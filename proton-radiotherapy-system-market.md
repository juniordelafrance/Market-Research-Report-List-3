<p><p>Proton radiotherapy systems are available in various types such as Synchrotron System, Cyclotron System, Synchrocyclotron System, and Others. The Synchrotron System utilizes a circular accelerator and magnetic fields to accelerate protons to high speeds. The Cyclotron System accelerates protons using a spiral path within a magnetic field. The Synchrocyclotron System combines features of both synchrotron and cyclotron systems. Other market types may include newer technologies or variations on existing systems for delivering proton therapy.</p></p>
