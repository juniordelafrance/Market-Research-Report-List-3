<p><p>Cell-Free DNA (cfDNA) Testing Market includes different types such as Donor-Derived Cell-Free DNA (DdcfDNA), Circulating Cell-Free Tumor DNA (CtDNA), and Cell-Free Fetal DNA (NIPT). DdcfDNA testing involves analyzing DNA from organ donors in transplant recipients. CtDNA testing looks for DNA fragments shed by tumors into the blood, aiding in cancer detection and monitoring. NIPT analyzes fetal DNA in a mother's blood to screen for genetic abnormalities during pregnancy. Each type of cfDNA testing serves a unique purpose in healthcare.</p></p>
