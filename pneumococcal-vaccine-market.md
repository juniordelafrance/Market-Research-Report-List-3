<p><p>Pneumococcal vaccine market includes PPSV 23, PCV 7/13, and PCV 10. PPSV 23 is a polysaccharide vaccine providing protection against 23 strains of pneumococcal bacteria. PCV 7/13 and PCV 10 are conjugate vaccines that protect against 7 and 13, or 10 strains of pneumococcal bacteria respectively. These vaccines are effective in preventing pneumococcal diseases such as pneumonia, meningitis, and sepsis. They are recommended for infants, young children, older adults, and individuals with certain medical conditions.</p></p>
