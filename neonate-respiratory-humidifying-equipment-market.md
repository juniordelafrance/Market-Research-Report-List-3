<p><p>Neonate respiratory humidifying equipment can be divided into two types: manual and automatic. Manual equipment requires the healthcare provider to adjust the settings and monitor the humidification levels regularly. Both types of equipment are crucial in providing optimal respiratory care for neonates, but automatic equipment offers more convenience and precision in maintaining the desired humidity levels.</p></p>
