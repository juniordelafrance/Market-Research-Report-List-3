<p><p>Direct-to-consumer Genetic Health Sequencing market includes different types such as Diagnostic Screening, Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD), and Relationship Testing. Diagnostic Screening involves testing for genetic diseases or predispositions, providing individuals with insight into their health risks. PGD is used for screening embryos for genetic disorders before implantation during IVF procedures. Relationship Testing determines genetic relationships between individuals, such as paternity or sibling verification. These services offer individuals the convenience of obtaining genetic information about their health and relationships directly.</p></p>
