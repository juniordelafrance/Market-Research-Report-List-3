<p><p>Microparticle injectables can be categorized into two main types: degradable and non-degradable. Degradable microparticles are designed to break down over time within the body, gradually releasing the medication or active ingredient they contain. On the other hand, non-degradable microparticles remain intact in the body without breaking down. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications, with degradable microparticles offering controlled release of drugs and non-degradable microparticles providing longer-lasting effects. Both types play a crucial role in the development of advanced drug delivery systems.</p></p>
