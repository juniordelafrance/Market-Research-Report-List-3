<p><p>Digital Marketing Software (DMS) is available in two main market types: Cloud-based and On-premise. Cloud-based DMS is hosted on remote servers and can be accessed through the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. On the other hand, On-premise DMS is installed and operated on the company's local servers, offering greater control over data security and customization. Both types cater to different business needs, with Cloud-based solutions being more popular for small to medium-sized businesses and On-premise solutions favored by larger enterprises.</p></p>
