<p><p>Surgical caps are available in two main market types: disposable and reusable. Disposable caps are intended for single-use only and are often made from lightweight materials such as paper or non-woven fabric. These caps are convenient for quick disposal after use, reducing the risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, reusable caps are designed to withstand multiple washings and are typically made from durable materials such as cotton or polyester. Reusable caps offer a more sustainable option for healthcare facilities looking to reduce waste and costs over time.</p></p>
