<p><strong>The Electrostrictive Stack Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-stres Electrostrictive Stack Actuator</li><li>No Pre-stress Electrostrictive Stack Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostrictive stack actuators are categorized into two main types: pre-stressed and no pre-stress. </p><p>Pre-stressed electrostrictive stack actuators are designed with an initial stress applied to the actuator material, which helps improve the actuator's performance and response time. On the other hand, no pre-stress electrostrictive stack actuators do not have any initial stress applied to the actuator material, resulting in different operating characteristics compared to pre-stressed actuators. Both types of actuators have their own unique advantages and applications in various industries such as robotics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
