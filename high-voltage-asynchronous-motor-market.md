<p><ul><li>Squirrel-cage</li><li>Slip-ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-voltage asynchronous motor market consists of two main types: squirrel-cage and slip-ring motors. Squirrel-cage motors are the most common and cost-effective option, consisting of a rotor with copper or aluminum bars running parallel to the motor shaft. Slip-ring motors, on the other hand, have a winding on the rotor connected to external resistance through slip rings and brushes, allowing for adjustable speed and torque. Both types are widely used in industrial applications requiring high power output and efficiency.</p></p>
