Serological and immunological assay involves the detection of complement proteins in blood samples. Molecular diagnostic tests utilize DNA analysis to identify genetic mutations associated with complement deficiencies. Immunohistochemistry techniques are used to visualize complement proteins in tissue samples. Other tests may include flow cytometry and functional assays to evaluate the activity of the complement system.</p></p>
