<p><p>Double diaphragm pumps are available in two main types - air operated and electrically operated. Air operated pumps are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for use in hazardous environments where electricity is not recommended. On the other hand, electrically operated pumps are powered by electricity and are more convenient to use in standard industrial settings. Both types of pumps offer efficient and reliable transfer of liquids, but the choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
