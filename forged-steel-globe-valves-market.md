<p><strong>The Forged Steel Globe Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bolted Bonnet</li><li>Welded Bonnet</li><li>Pressure Seal Bonnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Forged steel globe valves are available in three main types based on bonnet design: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. </p><p>Bolted bonnet valves are assembled by bolting the bonnet to the body, making them easy to maintain. </p><p>Welded bonnet valves have a welded seal between the bonnet and body, providing a more secure seal and preventing leakage. </p><p>Pressure seal bonnet valves are designed for high-pressure applications, featuring a bonnet that is sealed by pressure from the flow of the fluid.</p></p>
