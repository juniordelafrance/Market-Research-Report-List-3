<p><p>Triple Eccentric Butterfly Valves come in two main market types, Wafer Type and Lug Type. Wafer Type valves are designed to fit between two pipeline flanges and are held in place by bolts, while Lug Type valves have threaded inserts or lugs that allow them to be bolted to the pipeline. Both types are used in various industries for control and isolation of flowing media. Wafer Type valves are more compact and cost-effective, while Lug Type valves offer stronger anchoring and are preferred in applications with higher pressure or temperature requirements.</p></p>
