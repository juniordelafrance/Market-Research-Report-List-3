<p><p>The Decorative Floor Coating market includes two main types: Epoxy Decorative Floor Coating and Urethane Decorative Floor Coating. Epoxy coatings are known for their durability, chemical resistance, and high-gloss finish, making them popular for industrial and commercial applications.
