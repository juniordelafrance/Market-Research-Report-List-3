<p><p>Automatic wafer bonder market consists of two main types: semi-automated wafer bonder and fully-automated wafer bonder. Semi-automated wafer bonder requires some manual intervention for loading and unloading wafers, while the bonding process is automated. On the other hand, fully-automated wafer bonder operates without any manual intervention, from loading to bonding to unloading wafers. Both types offer increased efficiency and precision in semiconductor manufacturing, with fully-automated wafer bonder being the more advanced and streamlined option.</p></p>
