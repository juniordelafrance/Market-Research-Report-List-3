<p><p>Private deployment model in IoT device management involves organizations managing and controlling their own IoT devices within their own network. The public deployment model involves third-party service providers managing and controlling IoT devices on behalf of organizations. Hybrid deployment model is a combination of both, where organizations have control over some aspects of IoT device management while outsourcing others to third-party service providers. Each model offers different levels of control, flexibility, and security for managing IoT devices within an organization's network.</p></p>
