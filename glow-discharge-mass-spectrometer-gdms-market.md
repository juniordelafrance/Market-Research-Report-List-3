<p><p>Glow Discharge Mass Spectrometer (GDMS) is a powerful analytical tool used for elemental surface and depth profiling. There are two main types of GDMS: dc-GDMS and rf-GDMS. dc-GDMS utilizes a direct current discharge for analyzing samples, while rf-GDMS uses radio frequency for the same purpose. Both types offer high sensitivity, accuracy, and precision in measuring trace elements in solid samples. Researchers and industries use GDMS for a wide range of applications, including metallurgy, semiconductors, and environmental analysis.</p></p>
